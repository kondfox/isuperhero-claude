# iSuperhero — Game Overview

Source: https://www.isuperhero.ru/ (translated from Russian)

## What It Is

"I Am a Superhero" (Я — Супергерой) is a neuropsychological board game created by clinical psychologists and neuropsychologists. It develops cognitive and motor skills through story-based gameplay rather than traditional exercises.

## Target Audience

- Children ages 5–13 and their parents
- Specialists: neuropsychologists, educators, therapists
- Anyone interested in brain development through play

## What the Game Develops

- Inter-hemispheric brain communication
- Coordination and motor skills (fine and gross)
- Spatial awareness and left-right orientation
- Attention, memory, and thinking
- Self-esteem, confidence, and emotional regulation
- Brain activity and neural dynamics

## Physical Game Components

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| Rulebook + 5 instruction manuals | 250+ original story-based tasks |
| Game board | Double-sided, cosmic-themed |
| Superhero passports | 4 total (boy and girl versions) |
| Monster cards | 40 cards |
| Bonus cards | 40 cards |
| Accessories | 20-sided die, kinesiological ball, rope, markers, tokens, balloons, feather, erasable marker |

## Core Concept

Players are superheroes traveling through the cosmos. They develop five SuperAbilities by completing real-world tasks (physical, cognitive, creative), battle monsters by comparing ability scores, and win by taming three monsters on their ship. The game is designed so that the act of playing IS the brain training — no separate "exercise" step needed.

## Difficulty Levels

- **Level 1**: Ages 5–7
- **Level 2**: Ages 7–12
- **Level 3**: Ages 12+

## Session Flexibility

Games can be paused and resumed. State is preserved via passport markings, collected monsters, and earned bonus cards.
